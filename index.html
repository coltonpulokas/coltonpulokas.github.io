About Me:

Hey! My name is Colton Pulokas. I live in Hendersonville, and I'm a student at Jonathan Edwards Classical Academy. I am a nerd who enjoys learning about too many different things. I especially love to engage with controversial ideas and challenge the beliefs of everyone including my own.

The purpose of this site is to soon be a multi-threaded web server for my personal blog with comment functionality and a user database. Here I will post music and book reviews, software projects, and articles covering a wide variety of topics I find interesting. I am also planning to add a more casual page for posting photos, art, stories, dreams, etc which would function as a sort of decentralized social media page. My desire is to have a place where I can autonomously share my thoughts and creations on the internet without relying on third-party services!

This site is made entirely from HTML, CSS, and Rust. All code for this site is open source under the GPLv2 license and is available on my GitHub!
