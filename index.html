About Me:

My name is Colton Pulokas. I live in Hendersonville, and I'm a student at Jonathan Edwards Classical Academy. I enjoy learning about a wide variety of topics. I especially love discussing these topics with friends and telling about what I learn. My goal is to study economics in college and become a professor, author, and/or researcher.

This site is soon to be a multi-threaded web server for my personal blog with comment functionality and a user database. Here I will post music and book reviews, software projects, and articles covering a wide variety of topics I find interesting. I am also planning to add a more casual page for posting photos, art, stories, dreams, etc which would function as a sort of decentralized social media page. My desire is to have a place where I can autonomously share my thoughts and creations on the internet without relying on social media platforms! (I realize it is hosted on GitHub now, but in the future it will run on a home server or AWS server.)

This site is made entirely from HTML, CSS, and Rust. All code for this site is open source under the GPLv2 license and is available on my GitHub!
